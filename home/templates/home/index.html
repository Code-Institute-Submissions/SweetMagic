About Us

SweetMagic is a homemade, homebased bakery. Baking is our teams' passion and we take great pride in using only high quality ingredients on every one of our products. All of our desserts and cakes are fully customizable so if you wish to change anything in any of our products all you have do to is ask. Whether you wish to change the colour of a specific topping, change the theme of a any given cake or are allergic to an ingredient, we can do it, however, if that is the case you must first contact us using the form bellow and let us know what and how you wish the change the cake before placing the order. Furthermore, since we are, at this point, a small familly business we still don't offer any delivery option. That means that anything you order from us, you must pick up on a date and location to be agreed uppon via text. How does that work? Easy, just place your order, state in the "Notes" section the date you wish to pick it up and up till 2h after the order has been placed someone from our team will contact the number you attached to the order to set the pick up. We sincerely hope you will let us make your day sweetest!
